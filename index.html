Ayman Sadiq is the founder & CEO of 10 Minute School. This is the largest online education platform in Bangladesh which teaches more than 250,000 students every single day, that too completely free of cost.

10 Minute School has won many global accolades and a few to be mentioned would be The GLOMO Award in World Mobile Congress & The APICTA Award which is also known as the Oscars of ICT.

Ayman received The Queen’s Leader Award 2018 for creating access to quality education for millions of students in Bangladesh. He is also a One Young World ambassador & he recently got enlisted in the prestigious Forbe’s 30 Under 30 list.
